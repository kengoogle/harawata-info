Harawata Mochi Information

Takakura Shrine offers a special Japanese sweet called "Harawata Mochi," sold only on specific days known as "Doyo no Ushi" in the traditional Japanese calendar. This shrine is uniquely believed throughout Japan to have the power to alleviate stomach and intestinal discomfort. It's said that eating this mochi will keep your digestive system healthy for a year. One pack containing 6 pieces costs 450 yen (about 3-4 USD). We recommend trying it when you visit Japan. For hygiene reasons, please consume it on the day of purchase.
